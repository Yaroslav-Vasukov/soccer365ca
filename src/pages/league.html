Premier League

England • Founded 1992

The Premier League was established in 1992 as the top tier of English football, revolutionizing the sport with unprecedented commercial success and global reach.

From its inception with 22 founding clubs to becoming the most-watched football league worldwide, the Premier League has set new standards for competitive football and entertainment.

With over 3.2 billion viewers worldwide, the Premier League is the most-watched football league in the world.
